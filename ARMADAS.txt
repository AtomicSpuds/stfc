**Armada Prerequisites**

 Level 23

**Get started**

 Unlock Armadas
 build #Armada-control-center (ACC)

**Path 1: P2P**
  buy scopely pack and have what you need to unlock research and build building

**Path 2: F2P**
  complete events
  take part in Armadas from someone who has built the ACC
  collect Uncommon Armada Credit as rewards for winning
  spend Uncommon Armada Credit to get Armada Tactical Cores 
  use 1000 Tactical Cores to Unlock Armadas
  build ACC


**Using Armada**
 - one person starts an Armada, spends 100 Armada Directives
 - max ships 2-5, depending on leader's ACC level
 - others join the Armada
   - click link in alliance chat to see oragami Armada pin
   - select ship in drydock to participate
   - click on oragami Armada pin
   - click 'join'
   - ship will start warp to land near Armada pin
 - have a ship idle within the zone between start of attack and 15min timer
 - only one ship per player may participate
 - strongest ship per player in zone participates
 - strongest ships will be selected other players selecting 'join' will be
   backup
 - person starting Armada must be in the circle or it is canceled, no refund
   of Armada Directives
 - grey triangle indicas you joined but are either outside the circle or backup
 - pink triangle indicates you are currently joined and ready to rumble

**New Resources**
_Uncommon Armada Credit_
 - spent in the Alliance Store (see below)
 - acquired defeating Armada Targets
 - acquired purchasing an Armada pack for real money
_Armada Directive_
 - spent 100 at a time to create an Armada, pinned on a target
 - acquired through updating ACC
 - acquired by purhasing Armada Directives in Alliance Store
 - acquired by achieving 4th tier in dailies
    ops27 10 AD
_Armada Tactical Core_
 - 1000 required to Unlock Armada
 - to update to level 10 ACC you need 200, more each level after that
 - acquired by purchasing Armada Tactical
